Our most popular trip! Tackle exciting Class III-IV rapids with our expert guides. Ideal for thrill-seekers and those looking for a true whitewater experience.

For experienced rafters only! Conquer the wildest rapids on the river. High adrenaline, technical maneuvers, and unforgettable adventure await.
